# AI Disclosure

## AI Tools Used
- **Google Antigravity / AI coding assistant**: Used for code polishing, documentation scaffolding, UI improvement, and implementation planning.
- **ChatGPT**: Used for proposal drafting, pitch strategy, documentation wording, and competition positioning.
- **Gemini API**: Used for extracting information from resumes, analyzing job descriptions, and evaluating candidate readiness.
- **Pinecone**: Used for vector embedding and semantic matching of job opportunities.

## Purpose of AI Use
The AI models and tools are used to enhance the job matching experience, scaffold documentation, plan architecture, and power the generation of the Employer AI Interview Report.

## Human Review & Responsibility
All AI-assisted code, UI, and documentation are reviewed, tested, and owned by the team as part of the final submission responsibility.

The **TalentScreen AI Interview Report** is explicitly designed as a *decision-support tool* for Human Resources (HR) and hiring managers.
- It **does not** make final hiring decisions.
- It **does not** replace human recruiters or human judgement.
- It serves to highlight potential strengths, weaknesses, and skill gaps, but HR must manually review and further assess candidates.
