# TalentScreen AI - Q&A for Judges

### 1. How is this different from LinkedIn or JobStreet?
LinkedIn and JobStreet are primarily job boards and networking platforms. TalentScreen AI is an intelligent **screening layer**. Instead of just matching keywords, we provide actionable **Skill Gaps** for students to improve, and we conduct an **AI Interview** to give HR a deep, evidence-based readiness report before they even meet the candidate. 

### 2. Why would companies pay for this?
HR teams spend hundreds of hours screening candidates who look good on paper but lack communication or role-fit skills. By purchasing premium AI Interview Reports, employers get a standardized, structured evaluation of every candidate instantly, helping reduce early-stage screening time and improving review consistency.

### 3. How does the AI report help HR?
It acts as a powerful decision-support tool. The report breaks down the candidate's performance into communication, technical understanding, problem-solving, and role fit. Most importantly, it provides direct **evidence** from the interview and suggests tailored **follow-up questions** for the human HR manager to use in the final interview.

### 4. Why doesn't the AI replace human decision-making?
AI lacks the emotional intelligence and cultural nuance required to make a final hiring decision. We strictly position TalentScreen AI as a **decision-support** module. As stated in our Responsible AI Disclaimer, the final hiring decision must always be made by a human HR professional.

### 5. How are data privacy and API keys handled?
The system is designed to follow privacy-conscious practices. API keys are kept in backend environment variables and are not exposed to the frontend. Candidate data should only be used for the intended screening purpose.

### 6. What happens if the AI or API is slow during production?
We have implemented robust fallback mechanisms. If the live API times out or fails to fetch jobs, the system gracefully degrades by showing a friendly error message and providing a "Sample Recommendation" fallback, ensuring the user experience is never broken.

### 7. How does this fit into the Talentbank Career OS?
TalentScreen AI is designed as a **compatible module** for the Career OS. It enhances the existing ecosystem by adding an intelligent matching engine for the student portal and a premium B2B screening service for the employer portal, creating a new monetization channel while delivering immense value to both sides.
